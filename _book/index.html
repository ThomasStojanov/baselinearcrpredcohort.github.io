Characteristics and 2-year outcme data for the ARCR_Pred study: the Swiss-wide multicenter cohort for the evaluation and prediction of core outcomes in arthroscopic rotator cuff repair

T. Stojanov

2024-03-08

Chapter 1 About

This is a report example of a data science project written in Markdown. The ARCR_Pred study was initiated in June 2020. Last patient was recruited in November 2021.

The data comes from a REDCap database, designed for the purpose of the study. We needed about one year to monitor the data, using monitoring lists and resolve the opened data queries via our Electronic Data Capture (EDC) system.

More information can be found here: https://arcr-pred.ch and in Audigé et al. (2021).

References

Audigé, Laurent, Heiner C C Bucher, Soheila Aghlmandi, Thomas Stojanov, David Schwappach, Sabina Hunziker, Christian Candrian, et al. 2021. “Swiss-Wide Multicentre Evaluation and Prediction of Core Outcomes in Arthroscopic Rotator Cuff Repair: Protocol for the ARCR_pred Cohort Study.” BMJ Open 11 (4). https://doi.org/10.1136/bmjopen-2020-045702.
