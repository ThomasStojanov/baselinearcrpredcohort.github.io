3.3 Study representativity

We asked participating sites to provide us with a list of patients undergoing an arthroscopic rotator cuff repair (ARCR) over their own period of recruitment. The underlying idea was to assess our selection bias by comparing the population of enrolled patients to the population of not enrolled but eligible patients.

For all not enrolled patients, sites were asked to retrieve the following information, prospectively or retrospectively:

  • Sex
  • Year of birth
  • Tear severity

3.5 Attrition bias

We create a database to assess our attrition bias. We will focus on key selected variables, such as age, sex, tear severity and the OSS, reported in the questionnaire. We will therefore report on the fact that patients had or not a missed questionnaire at a specific time point and we generate the variable called missing_6MO, missing_12MO and missing_24MO. We will compare variables distribution across values for these variables.

datamissing <- data |>

    ptn,
    event,
    Age,
    gender,
    tearsev,
